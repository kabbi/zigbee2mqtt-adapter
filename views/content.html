Updating. Please wait 10 minutes before updating another device.

Here you can find out how your Zigbee devices have organised themselves into a network. Depending on the size of your network, updating the map can take up to two minutes. During this time your Zigbee network may be very unresponsive.

The addon will have generated a random network ID and network password when it was installed. You might want to write these down. Whenever these are changed, you will need to re-pair all your devices.







How to use the Zigbee2MQTT adapter

Zigbee2MQTT is an open source software project in which hundreds of people have worked together to support as many Zigbee devices as possible. This addon builds on their work.

To learn more about the project, and learn which devices are supported, visit www.zigbee2mqtt.io.

 

A word about firmware updates

This addon can help you keep your devices up to date with the latest firmware. Not all manufacturers support this, but some do. IKEA, for example.

Keeping Zigbee devices up to date isn't vital. Since Zigbee devices do not connect to the internet by themselves, they less likely to be hacked.

Before you update a device, always make sure you do some research. While firmware updates should generally improve security and perhaps even add features, in some cases there may be downsides associated with updating firmware on your devices.

Any interruption of the update process could damage your device. It's recommended to install fresh batteries in a device before you start a firmware update. Also make sure you don't turn off your Webthings Gateway during the proces.

This addon is not associated with any manufacturer of Zigbee devices. Use the update functionality at your own risk.
